At SoftConstruct, I led the development of **AJNA**, an AI-powered sports tracking tool. The system stitched two 8K camera feeds into a panoramic video and used computer vision to track the ball, referees and players without human intervention. It delivered realistic HD broadcasts with augmented visualizations.
